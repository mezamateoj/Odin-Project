Tomato Soup

Description

One spoonful of tomato soup has the power to automatically transport you back to your childhood. The tasty soup is the ultimate comfort food, especially when it's homemade.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups chopped fresh tomatoes
  • 1 slice of onion
  • 4 gloves of garlic

Directions

  1. In a stockpot, over medium heat, combine the tomatoes, large slice of onion, garlic cloves, and chicken broth. Bring to a boil, and gently boil for about 20 minutes to blend all of the flavors

  2. In the now empty stockpot, melt the butter over medium heat. Stir in the flour to make a roux, cooking until the roux is a medium brown
